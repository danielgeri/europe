Family Trip

Overview & Approach

Moderate pace: 1–2 main activities per day, afternoon downtime for naps, stroller-friendly routes where possible. Meals include 2–3 restaurant options per meal so you can choose by mood.

Family profile: mother-in-law (picky), two families, one 4‑year‑old, two 1‑year‑olds. Restaurants flagged for kid-friendliness and reservation tips.

Trip Calendar — Day-by-day

Fri, Aug 29 — Travel
SFO → Paris (CDG). United Airlines MZXXVE • Departs 2:40 PM
  • Tip: bulkhead seats help with toddlers and diaper changes.
Sat, Aug 30 — Arrival & Saint‑Germain (Paris)
Land CDG 10:25 AM → hotel: 9 Rue Racine (6th Arr.)
  • Morning: check-in / Luxembourg Gardens (playground, puppet theater).
  • Lunch options:
    • Le Relais Saint‑Germain / Le Comptoir — reliable bistro, near your hotel; easy for picky eaters.

Sun, Aug 31 — Louvre / Montmartre (Paris)
Classic things with optional museum time; keep it flexible for naps.
  • Morning: Louvre courtyard & Tuileries (or Musée d'Orsay if preferred).
  • Lunch options: Café Marly (scenic) or Le Fumoir.
  • Afternoon: Montmartre / Sacré‑Coeur (funicular) or hotel rest.
  • Dinner: Seine cruise + casual dinner OR neighborhood bistro (La Jacobine).
Mon, Sept 1 — Birthday day (Paris)
Slow morning, optional light activity. Special dinner: upscale-but-relaxed.
  • Morning & afternoon: relaxed — pastry breakfast, light stroll or private small activity (Seine cruise or pastry workshop).
  • Birthday Dinner — Recommended: Les Ombres (rooftop with Eiffel Tower view). Book window table, mention birthday and toddlers.
  • Alternatives: Le Coupe‑Chou (cosy) or a small fine bistro for intimacy.
Tue, Sept 2 — Paris → Hendaye (TGV) → San Sebastián
TGV departs 12:11 PM • Arrives Hendaye 4:47 PM • Local train to San Sebastián
  • Check-in: Easo Kalea, 27 (Old Town).
  • Dinner: Casa Gandarias (sit-down pintxos) or Bodegón Alejandro (traditional Basque cooking).
Wed, Sept 3 — San Sebastián (La Concha & Parte Vieja)
Beach morning + Old Town pintxos.
  • Morning: La Concha promenade, playground.
  • Lunch options: Bar Nestor (tomato, steak, tortilla — small; go early) or Gandarias.
  • Afternoon: Monte Urgull walk or hotel nap.
  • Dinner: La Viña (famous cheesecake) or Bodegón Alejandro.
Thu, Sept 4 — San Sebastián (market + Aquarium)
Market visit and calm afternoon.
  • Morning: Mercado de la Bretxa (San Martín Market).
  • Lunch: market cafés or Bar La Cepa.
  • Afternoon: Aquarium or Ondarreta beach.
  • Dinner: Casa Urola or similar Old Town sit-down.
Fri, Sept 5 — Day trip: Biarritz or Getaria
Choose based on preferred vibe: dramatic coastline (Biarritz) or peaceful fishing village (Getaria).
  • Getaria: excellent waterfront grilled fish restaurants.
  • Biarritz: seaside promenade and brasseries near the casino.
  • Tip: consider renting a car for flexibility with toddlers.
Sat, Sept 6 — San Sebastián (final day)
Last strolls and favorite pintxos; pack.
  • Morning: final pintxos crawl / souvenir shopping in Parte Vieja.
  • Lunch: repeat a favorite (Bar Nestor or Gandarias).
Sun, Sept 7 — Fly to Barcelona
Vueling VY2487 • Departs 5:55 PM • Arrives BCN 7:00 PM • Lodging: Avinguda Diagonal, 438
  • Evening dinner options: Cervecería Catalana (tapas) or Can Culleretes (historic Catalan)
Mon, Sept 8 — Barcelona (Sagrada Família & Park Güell)
Gaudí day — book tickets in advance; early morning for Sagrada Família.
  • Lunch: La Paradeta (seafood market style) or Tapaç 24.
  • Dinner: Cinc Sentits (upscale tasting) or Cal Pep.
Tue, Sept 9 — Gothic Quarter & Barceloneta
La Boqueria morning + beach afternoon.
  • Lunch: El Xampanyet, Quimet & Quimet (fast, tasty tapas).
  • Dinner: Can Majó (seafood by the water) or Bodega 1900.
Wed, Sept 10 — Flex day (Barcelona)
Visit favorites again or choose a family activity: Aquarium, bike ride, cooking class.
Thu, Sept 11 — Barcelona → SFO
Iberia J5BFD • Departs 2:05 PM — allow airport time for toddlers.

Important Reservations & Notes

  • Les Ombres — birthday rooftop in Paris: request Eiffel-facing table; book early.
  • Arzak (San Sebastián) — iconic if you want a top culinary experience; reserve well in advance.
  • Cinc Sentits (Barcelona) — reserve for tasting menu if you want a special meal.
  • For pintxos bars (Bar Nestor, Casa Gandarias, La Viña) go early or choose midday when less crowded.
  • Always request high chairs and note toddlers when booking.
